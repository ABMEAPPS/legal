Privacy Policy A Better Me Apps · Effective March 22, 2026 Who We Are A Better Me Apps ("ABME," "we," "us") develops health and lifestyle tracking applications. Our apps include Healthy Heart, Goal Master, No Home Gym, Kitchen Chef, and V-Diary, with additional apps in development. Your Data Stays on Your Device All health and personal data you enter into an ABME app is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your health data on any server. Your readings, medications, meals, exercises, and other entries never leave your phone, tablet, or computer unless you choose to export or share them. What We Do Collect If you contact us by text message, email, or through our website, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, phone number, email address, and which apps you expressed interest in. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and send you the materials you requested. Text Messaging If you text our phone number to request information about our apps, we will respond with the materials you requested. We do not send unsolicited text messages. You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your requests. Email Communications If you provide your email address, we may send you information about the apps you requested and occasional updates about new features or apps. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in every email. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services to operate our platform: Twilio — processes text messages sent to and from our phone number. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy. GitHub Pages — hosts our app files as static web pages. GitHub does not have access to any data you enter into our apps. Cloudflare — provides our license management and AI help assistant features. Only license status and configuration data pass through Cloudflare — never your health data. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Provider-Configured Apps If your healthcare provider configures an ABME app for your use, the provider may set your display name, clinical targets, and enabled features. Your health data remains on your device. The provider does not have remote access to the data you enter — you share it with them by generating a report within the app. Data Export and Deletion You may export your data at any time using the export features within each app. You may delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's stored data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app. Children's Privacy Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
